Code of Conduct

The FOSSASIA community is made up of members from around the globe with a diverse set of skills, personalities, and experiences. It is through these differences that our community experiences great successes and continued growth. When you're working with members of the community, we encourage you to follow these guidelines which help steer our interactions and strive to keep FOSSASIA a positive, successful, and growing community. You find our code of conduct at http://coc.fossasia.org
